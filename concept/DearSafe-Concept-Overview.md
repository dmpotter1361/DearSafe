# DearSafe 🔒💗
### A private, pretty place for your life and your projects

*(working name — could change)*

---

## What it is, in one breath

DearSafe is a journaling app that's **locked tight, lovely to look at, and built around
pictures as much as words.** Think of it as a "dear diary" that's also a project notebook —
somewhere you can pour out your day *and* document your apple tree through the seasons, a
woodworking build from rough cut to final stain, or the exact recipe for your last soap
batch.

It lives in your web browser and on your phone, so you can write from your computer at night
and snap a photo from the garden in the morning — same journal, everywhere.

---

## What it feels like to use

**📖 Write like you would in Word.**
Bold, italics, underline, colors, headings, lists, different fonts — all the formatting you'd
expect, plus emoji 😊🌸. Nothing stiff or plain.

**🖼️ Pictures live *inside* your writing.**
Drop a photo right into the middle of a sentence, add a caption, keep typing. Scrolling back
through an entry feels like flipping through a storyboard — text and pictures telling the
story together, in order. You can add small videos and voice notes too.

**🎨 Dress up every entry.**
Beach day? Give that entry a soft, beachy background. Cozy rainy afternoon? There's a theme
for that. Pick backgrounds, accent colors, and fonts per entry — make each day feel like the
day it was. There's a soft **dark mode** for night journaling, too.

**🔒 Truly private.**
Everything is locked behind your password and **scrambled (encrypted)** so that even if
someone got hold of the files, they'd see nothing but gibberish. No one — not even whoever
runs the server — can read your entries. It locks itself when you step away.

**🔎 Find anything, fast.**
Remember writing about a specific brand of wax months ago but not the date? Just search. It
looks through your words, your tags, and (later) even text *inside your photos*.

**🏷️ Organize the natural way.**
Instead of rigid folders, you add little **tags** — `#garden`, `#woodworking`, `#gifts`. An
entry can have several, so it's easy to later pull up, say, everything you ever made as a gift.

**📝 Never face a blank page.**
One-click **templates** get you started — and you can make your own. A "Soap Batch" template
with your ingredient ratios and temperatures, a "Daily Standup" for household plans, whatever
you reuse.

**📅 Knows the date, keeps your calendar in view.**
Entries are automatically dated. A calendar and timeline let you wander back through your
memories, and it can show what was on your calendar that day for context.

---

## A couple of honest notes (worth knowing up front)

- **The lock has no spare key.** Because it's genuinely secure, if you ever lost *both* your
  password *and* your printed recovery card, not even we could get your entries back. That's
  the trade for real privacy. (Setup walks you through saving a recovery card so this stays a
  "what if," not a "uh oh.")
- **Videos go in your own Google Drive.** Videos are big, so to keep things tidy they're
  stored — still scrambled and private — in your own Google Drive rather than piling up on the
  server. Photos and voice notes stay right in the app.
